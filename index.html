About AI

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. AI is the science of mankind. It can do things that are considered "smart." AI technology can process large amounts of data in ways unlike humans. The goal for AI is to be able to do things such as recognize patterns, make decisions, and judge like humans. AI is specially designed to help humans with their work and boost productivity.
